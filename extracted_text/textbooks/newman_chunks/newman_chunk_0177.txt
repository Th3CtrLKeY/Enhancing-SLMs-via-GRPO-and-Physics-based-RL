The problem statement is completed by requiring that Laplace’s equation must be satisfied in the fluid, the pertur­ bation velocity and pressure should vanish at infinity, and the velocity at the trailing edge of the foil is finite. The latter condition, like the Kutta condition for the fully wetted lifting foil, is necessary to ensure a smooth transition of the flow from the body surface to the cavity, with continuous slope between these two boundaries.
