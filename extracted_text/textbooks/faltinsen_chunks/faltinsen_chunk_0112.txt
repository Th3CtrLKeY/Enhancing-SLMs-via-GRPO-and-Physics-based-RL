separation occurs with the experimental values observed by Zhao et al.
(1988). They investigated a hemisphere of diameter 1 m. In calm water
the centre of the hemisphere was at the still water free-surface level.
Visualization tests were performed to study the occurrence of flow
separation around the hemisphere in combined current and regular
waves. The model was restrained from oscillating. Only the flow at the
